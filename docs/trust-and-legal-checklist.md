# Trust And Legal Checklist

The public site must not invent legal/operator details. The operator should provide and verify these items before they are published.

## Operator Details To Provide

- Legal operator name
- Serviceable postal address, if required
- Public contact email or approved contact form policy
- Responsible person for editorial/legal notices
- Privacy controller information
- Cookie and tracking summary
- Community moderation policy
- Job listing verification and takedown policy
- Report, correction, and deletion flow
- Escalation path for rights complaints or safety issues

## Publication Rules

- Publish exact legal details only after operator confirmation.
- Keep unverified job examples out of sitemap and search.
- Keep static directory seed cards generic; they may describe how to search or verify a listing, but must not imply a real shop, clinic, employer, or provider exists.
- Explain that Community transactions are user responsibility and recommend public handover locations.
- Explain that Medical pages are not medical advice and emergency users should contact official emergency services.
- Explain on Jobs surfaces that applicants must verify the employer/listing source, contract terms, salary, visa support, and application destination.
- Keep Contact, Privacy, Terms, and Impressum copy polished, factual, and free of placeholder labels such as `TODO`, `TBD`, or generic administrator names.
